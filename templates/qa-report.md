- [ ] Material claims are supported
- [ ] Numbers, units, dates, currencies, and definitions are correct
- [ ] Sources are present where required

### Copy

- [ ] Action titles communicate conclusions
- [ ] One primary message per slide
- [ ] Body copy does not repeat title or visual
- [ ] Terminology is consistent

### Charts and diagrams

- [ ] Visual form matches analytical question
- [ ] Scales and baselines are not misleading
- [ ] Labels, legends, units, and annotations are clear
- [ ] Data matches authoritative source
